# Writing Style

Use the user's voice. Keep messages concise and human.

## Outgoing Messages

- First person when the message is sent from the user.
- No fabricated claims.
- No over-formal corporate filler.
- Avoid obvious AI phrasing.
- Do not tell contacts a resume is tailored or customized.
- Mention "my resume" or "resume" instead.
- Keep referral DMs and follow-ups short.

## Approval

Show externally visible drafts before sending unless the user has explicitly asked for an autonomous safe mode for that exact action.

## Resume And Cover Letter

- Truthful, role-specific, and concise.
- Match the user's existing tone where possible.
- Do not invent metrics or experience.
- Cover letters are generated only when required or explicitly requested.
